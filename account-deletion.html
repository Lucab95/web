Account & Data Deletion

Cashflow Calendar
Last updated: 2026-02-15

How to request deletion

Send an email to support@<your-domain> with subject Account deletion request from your account email.

What is deleted

  • Authentication account
  • Cloud-stored app data tied to that account
  • Associated cloud files owned by the account (when applicable)

Processing timeline

Target processing time: within 30 days after verification.

Notes

Local data already on your device may remain until app data is cleared or the app is uninstalled.
