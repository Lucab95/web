Deletion requests

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated cloud data.

Self-service deletion (in-app)

If you are signed in, you can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the Profile tab
  2. Tap Delete account
  3. Confirm the deletion

This will immediately delete your authentication account and all cloud data tied to it.

How to request deletion

Send an email to luca.brugaletta@outlook.com with subject Account deletion request from your account email address.

Include:

  • The email address used in the app
  • Confirmation that you want account and cloud data deleted

What is deleted

  • Authentication account
  • Cloud-stored app data tied to that account
  • Associated cloud files owned by the account (when applicable)

Processing timeline

Self-service deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed within 30 days after verification.

Verification

Self-service deletion uses your active sign-in session. For email requests, we may verify account ownership before deleting data.

Notes

Local data already on your device may remain until app data is cleared or the app is uninstalled.
