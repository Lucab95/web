Privacy

This Privacy Policy explains how Cashflow Calendar processes information to provide subscription tracking, reminders, and account features.

1) Data we process

  • Account information (email and sign-in provider identifiers)
  • User-provided content (subscriptions, one-time entries, app preferences)
  • Technical and diagnostics data required to operate and secure the app

2) Why we process data

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Enable cross-device synchronization when cloud sync is active
  • Support authentication, account integrity, and abuse prevention

3) Storage and processors

Data is stored locally on your device and, when enabled, in cloud services used by the app (for example Firebase services configured in production).

4) Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is only shared with infrastructure providers required to deliver app functionality.

5) Retention and deletion

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You can request deletion of your account and associated cloud data via the Account & Data Deletion page.

6) Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational controls, including authenticated access to user-scoped cloud data.

7) International transfers

Service providers may process data in regions where they operate infrastructure.

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this page to reflect product or legal changes. The date at the top indicates the latest revision.
