Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-15

This Privacy Policy explains how Cashflow Calendar processes data to provide subscription tracking, reminders, and account features.

  • Account info (email/provider identifiers)

2) Why we process data

  • Core app functionality

3) Storage and processors

Data is stored locally on your device and, when enabled, in cloud services (e.g. Firebase components configured by the app).

4) Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app.

5) Retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated cloud data. See Account Deletion.
